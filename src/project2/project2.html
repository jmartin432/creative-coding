Video Capture

  • This demo uses the webcam to capture video.
  • A selection of pixels from the webcam capture are chosen depending on the selected radius.
  • From that selection of pixels, either the Red, Green, Blue, Brightness is used to determine the fill color for a circle centered at the same location as the corresponding pixel.
  • Radius, Pixel Color Value and Radius Color can all be chosen below.
